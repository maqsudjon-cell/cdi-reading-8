Reading Test Instructions

Read carefully before you begin. The 60-minute timer starts when you click Begin Test.

  • Read 3 passages and answer 40 questions in 60 minutes.
  • The passage is on the left; questions on the right. Drag the centre divider to resize.
  • Select text, then click the floating Highlight button. Click a highlight to remove it.
  • Use the Next → arrow or a question number in the footer to move. Switch passages with the passage cells.
  • Spell-check is disabled on inputs, as in the real exam.
  • The timer turns orange at 10 min and flashes red at 5 min. Auto-submits at 0:00.
  • After the last question, a Review screen lets you return before final submission.
